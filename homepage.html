THE ROOTS

It all began in 1945, after the liberation of Florence. Renzo Salvadori, fifteen years old, is hired at a small mechanical workshop in the center of Florence. Here the young Renzo learns to operate the lathe and discovers his genius as a fountain pen maker.

Significance and Importance

The vintage fountain pen holds a special place in history and culture. Once a staple tool for writing and communication, it represented elegance, status, and craftsmanship. Fountain pens were often used by intellectuals, writers, and professionals, lending an air of authority to their words. Today, vintage fountain pens are cherished not only for their functionality but also as collectible items, treasured for their beauty and nostalgia.
